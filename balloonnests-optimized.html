About the studio

Built by hand,
every single time.

Balloon Nests is a small, appointment-only balloon studio specializing in custom installations for weddings, birthdays, and milestone celebrations.

Every piece is designed from scratch around your colour palette, venue dimensions, and the feeling you want guests to walk into. We use professional-grade latex and foil balloons, build on-site, and stay until everything is placed and styled. After the event, we return for full takedown and cleanup.

We work with couples, party planners, corporate teams, and anyone who wants balloon decor that feels intentional rather than inflated. Our studio is open Tuesday to Saturday by appointment, and we deliver across the greater metro area with weekend travel available for destination events.

Common questions

Everything you need
to know.

Table centerpieces start at $45, balloon gifts from $79, arches from $189, and mosaic numbers from $219. Custom installations are quoted per project based on size, complexity, and venue. All prices include delivery, on-site setup, styling, and post-event takedown.

We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for standard pieces like centerpieces and gifts, and 4 weeks for large custom installations, arches, or multi-piece setups. We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours with a custom proposal tailored to your event.

Yes, every order includes delivery, on-site setup, and styling at your venue. After your event, we come back and take everything down. You celebrate, we handle the logistics. Delivery is included for venues within the greater metro area, with weekend travel available for destination events.

We create balloon installations for weddings, birthdays, baby showers, anniversaries, graduations, corporate events, brand launches, holiday parties, gender reveals, and proposals. If you have a celebration in mind, we can design something for it.

Absolutely. Every installation is designed around your colour palette, theme, and venue. Share your inspiration, mood board, fabric swatches, or Pantone colours and we match them precisely. We carry over 80 balloon colours and can mix custom shades.

Our installations are built to last the full duration of your event, typically 8 to 12 hours indoors. We use professional-grade balloons treated for maximum longevity. Outdoor installations may vary depending on heat and sun exposure. For multi-day events, we can refresh or rebuild pieces as needed.

We serve the greater metro area for standard delivery and setup. For venues further afield, we offer weekend travel for an additional fee. Contact us with your venue location and event date, and we will confirm availability and any travel costs.
